About Us

Founded in 2016 by five university friends in Uttar Pradesh (India), VARUS has since become a leading global accommodation search website. We are focused on reshaping the way millions of travelers search for and compare hotels and other accommodations. Part of Expedia Group (NASDAQ: TRVG), VARUS’ mission is to become your companion to experience our world. We are dedicated to provide you the best possible deals on hotel bookings all around the globe with an additional offer of guaranteed exciting discounts.
